Alcohol Comsuption Around the World

Using data comprised by Matt OP a Data Engineer from Philadelphia, United States, this visualisation explores the consumption of alcohol in countries around the world. Based in 2016, the data is measured in Litres (L) Per Capita.

Alcohol is consumed all around the world!

Most countries drink, but some don't!

With Europe doing most of the Drinking,

And Nothern Africa doing the least.

Projections show that moving through the years the total alcohol consumed, whilst only in a small amount, will continue to grow through the years! Most Countries will follow the same trend, but there are a few outliers such as the Cook Islands and Esontia! Search them up below to see their trends!
